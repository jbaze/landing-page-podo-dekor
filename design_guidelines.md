# Pododekor Landing Page Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach** inspired by professional construction industry websites, drawing from companies like BuildDirect, Home Depot Pro, and European construction material suppliers. Focus on trust, professionalism, and industrial strength.

## Core Design Elements

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: 220 85% 25% (Deep construction blue)
- **Secondary**: 220 15% 20% (Charcoal gray)
- **Accent**: 35 95% 55% (Construction orange - used sparingly)
- **Neutral Light**: 220 10% 95% (Off-white backgrounds)
- **Neutral Dark**: 220 20% 15% (Dark text)

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts) - clean, professional sans-serif
- **Headings**: Bold weights (700-800) for impact
- **Body**: Regular (400) and medium (500) weights
- **Scale**: Large headings (3xl-4xl) for hero, standard sizing (base-lg) for content

### Layout System
Tailwind spacing primitives: **4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 24** units
- Consistent 6-unit gaps between sections
- 4-unit padding for cards and buttons
- 12-16 unit margins for major layout elements

## Component Library

### Navigation
Clean header with company logo, minimal navigation menu, and prominent contact button in construction orange

### Hero Section
Full-width hero with construction imagery background, overlay text, and two CTA buttons (primary filled, secondary outline with blurred background)

### Service Cards
Grid layout showcasing key services (Dry Construction, Laminate Flooring, Facade Materials) with icons and brief descriptions

### Contact Section
Professional contact form with company details, business hours, and location information

### Trust Elements
Authorized dealer badges and partnership logos (KRONOSWISS, WEBER) to build credibility

## Content Sections (Maximum 4)
1. **Hero** - Company introduction and primary CTAs
2. **Services** - Key service categories with visual cards
3. **About/Trust** - Company credentials and partnerships
4. **Contact** - Contact form and business information

## Visual Treatment
- **Professional Construction Aesthetic**: Clean lines, industrial feel
- **Subtle Gradients**: Light blue-to-white gradients in hero background
- **High Contrast**: Strong contrast between text and backgrounds for readability
- **Minimal Animations**: Subtle hover effects only, no distracting motions

## Images
- **Large Hero Image**: Construction site or building materials warehouse scene with professional workers
- **Service Icons**: Simple line icons for dry construction, flooring, and facade work
- **Partnership Logos**: KRONOSWISS and WEBER brand logos in trust section
- **Background Textures**: Subtle concrete or material textures for section dividers

## Language & Content
- **Primary Language**: Macedonian (with Cyrillic script support)
- **Professional Tone**: Authoritative, trustworthy, industry-focused
- **Clear Hierarchy**: Prominent headings, scannable content structure
